Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice

This is a classic college food dish for the lazy people that don't know how to cook like me!

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Rice
  • Bacon
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

  1. Spray cooking oil on pan
  2. Fry bacon in pan for 2 min
  3. Add Kimchi and fry for a few more minutes
  4. Fry rice for 2 minutes after all other ingredients look cooked
  5. Enjoy!
