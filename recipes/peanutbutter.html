PB Sandwich

The perfect PB Sandwich!

This is the perfect PB sandwich, a great quick fix meal

Ingredients

  • peanut butter (no shit)
  • bread
  • love
  1. Take bread out of the bag
  2. Open peanut butter jar lid
  3. With a knife gently rub that peanut butter all over the bread
  4. Spread with love
  5. Enjoy!
